# .impeccable.md — Design Context

This file persists design guidelines for the **Simulador da Reforma Tributária** project.
It is automatically referenced by AI assistants for all design decisions.

---

## Design Context

### Users

**Primary persona: João (MEI)** — a microentrepreneur with no accounting background, using the tool on a mobile phone, under time pressure. He wants a yes/no answer in under 60 seconds: "Will the tax reform raise my taxes?" He does not want forms, jargon, NCM codes, or login walls.

Secondary personas: Ana (Simples Nacional salon owner) who wants year-by-year projections; Carlos (Lucro Presumido) who needs a detailed analysis; Marina (accountant) who shares results with clients.

**Job to be done:** Understand the personal fiscal impact of Brazil's 2026–2033 tax reform quickly, without needing an accountant.

**Context of use:** Mobile-first, often low digital literacy, high fiscal anxiety. The user arrives skeptical — every other tool has a lead-capture form. This one must earn trust immediately.

---

### Brand Personality

**Three words: Acessível · Direto · Honesto**
(Accessible · Direct · Honest)

- **Acessível** — plain Portuguese, no legalese, emoji-assisted option labels, 60-second promise.
- **Direto** — no marketing fluff, no lead-gen friction. Data first, call-to-action second.
- **Honesto** — mandatory disclaimers, "estimate" framing, deliberately neutral on the politics of the reform.

**Emotional goal:** After using the simulator, the user should feel **informed confidence** — "I now understand exactly how this affects me."

**Voice:** That of a knowledgeable friend who happens to be an accountant. Never condescending. Never selling anything.

---

### Aesthetic Direction

**Visual metaphor: "Civic Fintech"** — the intersection of an official Brazilian civic document (credibility, authority, flat structure) and a modern fintech data tool (Nubank / Guiabolso clarity, whitespace, purposeful color).

**References:**
- ✅ Nubank / Guiabolso — clean, high-contrast, information-dense without being cold, restrained color palette
- ✅ High-quality civic portals — structural trust signals (seal, disclaimer, neutral tone)

**Anti-references:**
- ❌ No lead-gen landing pages — no hero CTAs that feel like conversion funnels
- ❌ No generic SaaS dashboards — no blue gradient everything, no "your growth metrics" aesthetics
- ❌ No playful / gamified UI — no cartoon avatars, no confetti, no bright accent overload
- ❌ No heavy government bureaucracy — no dense grey portals, no wall-of-text forms

**Color system:**
- Navy `#001E40` — authority, institutional trust (nav, footer, primary buttons, headings)
- Emerald `#006C4B` — forward-looking growth, action, CTAs
- Warm off-white `#FBF9F9` — "paper document" background, never pure white
- Slate blue `#5E6F83` — neutral data, secondary info, current-tax chart
- Mint `#9AF5CA` / `#057351` — icon container highlights only; never overused

**Typography:** Public Sans — chosen for its civic/government readability at all weights. Headlines: extrabold, tight tracking. Body: regular, relaxed leading. Numbers/years: monospace.

**Theme:** Light mode only. No dark variant. The official-document metaphor requires paper-like warmth (`#FBF9F9` background) that dark mode undermines.

**Shape language:** Flat cards, `rounded-md` (8px), no decorative drop shadows. Borders are `#C3C6D1` at low opacity. Elevation is communicated through background contrast, not shadows.

---

### Design Principles

1. **Trust before delight.** Every visual decision must first signal credibility and neutrality. Delight is earned — never imposed. Avoid anything that feels like marketing.

2. **Data is the hero.** Numbers, charts, and year projections are the primary content. UI chrome exists to frame and clarify data, not to compete with it.

3. **Accessible to João, useful to Marina.** Design for the lowest-literacy persona first. If João can't parse it in 60 seconds on a phone, it's too complex. But don't dumb down — accountants should find enough depth to share confidently.

4. **Civic restraint.** The palette is intentionally narrow. Resist adding colors, gradients, or decorative elements. Each color has a defined semantic role — do not reassign them casually.

5. **Honest friction.** The wizard flow should feel effortless but not frictionless to the point of seeming trivial. Three clear steps, explicit disclaimers, and "estimate" language preserve the intellectual honesty that differentiates this tool.

/* No decorative box-shadows */
